Guru nanak girls college

Established in 1970, Guru Nanak Girls College, commonly known as GNGC is affiliated with Punjab University. It is located in Ludhiana, Punjab and is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC). The college is also accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
